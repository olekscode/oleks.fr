I am a computer science researcher working at the intersection of participatory modelling, serious games, and scientific software.

My work combines research questions about modelling and collaboration with the design of software systems that make those questions operational. I am especially interested in tools that help researchers, practitioners, and participants reason together about complex socio-ecological systems.

This website is meant to be both a professional reference point and a place for writing. It presents publications, projects, and a small amount of blog content, while keeping the overall structure clear and maintainable.
